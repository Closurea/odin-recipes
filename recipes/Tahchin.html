Tahchin

Tahchin is a savory Persian rice cake, made with layers of rice, yogurt, saffron, and tender chicken, all baked to a golden, crispy perfection.

It's a beloved comfort food that’s both fragrant and flavorful, offering a delicious balance of textures.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice (washed)
  • 500g chicken breast (cooked and shredded)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1/4 tsp saffron (dissolved in hot water)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1/4 cup barberries (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook Rice: Parboil the rice in salted water for about 7-8 minutes, drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare Chicken: Cook the chicken with chopped onion, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Shred the chicken once cooked.
  3. Make Yogurt Mixture: In a bowl, combine yogurt, eggs, saffron water, and melted butter or oil.
  4. Mix: Combine the parboiled rice, shredded chicken, and yogurt mixture, mixing well.
  5. Layer: Grease a baking dish and layer the rice mixture. Press down to compact.
  6. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 1 hour. Remove foil for the last 10-15 minutes to crisp the top.
  7. Serve: Garnish with barberries (optional) and serve hot.
